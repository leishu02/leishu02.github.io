About

My name is 舒蕾. I am a Research Scientist at Google DeepMind, specializing in Agentic AI and Coding. I lead Project Astra 3D and am a core contributor to Project Astra and Gemini.

Prior to this, I worked at Amazon AWS AI and Amazon Alexa AI. In 2020, I earned my Ph.D. in Computer Science at the University of Illinois at Chicago under Prof. Bing Liu's supervision. I also worked as a research intern at Uber AI Lab, where I was advised by Dr. Piero Molino and Dr. Gokhan Tur. I am a winner of the Yelp Dataset Challenge.

Research Focus

  • Agentic AI & RL — harness/loop engineering and self-evolving harnesses, multi-agent systems, agentic RL, and cross-device (server, phone, glass) agents.
  • Coding — improve Gemini Vibe and Agentic Coding capability including agentic procedural 3D modeling via code.
