Hello :)

my name is 舒蕾. I am an Applied Scientist at Amazon Alexa AI. Previously, I received my Ph.D. in Computer Science at University of Illinois at Chicago under Prof. Bing Liu's supervision. I work with my advisor on sentiment analysis and lifelong learning. I also collaborate with Dr. Piero Molino (Stanford University) and Dr. Gokhan Tur (Amazon Alexa AI) on Conversational AI, NLU/NLG research. I closely work with Hu Xu (FAIR) on representation learning (language modeling, word embedding), open-world learning, meta learning and question answering.
